Web developer and Designer

Hey there! I’m David, a graphic designer and web developer who’s passionate about creating beautiful, functional designs. I’ve been learning to code for the last 4 years. I started out as a lost and naive boy with a dream, and now I’m here, blending creativity with technical skills to bring ideas to life. I work with a wide range of tools and technologies, from Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma for design, to HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python for web development. Whether I’m designing a logo, creating a brand identity, or building responsive websites and interactive user interfaces, I strive to make things that are not only visually appealing but also user-friendly and functional. For me, design is about problem-solving. It’s about creating experiences that are intuitive and enjoyable for users, while ensuring they look amazing. I love combining my design background with my web development knowledge to create cohesive digital solutions. Teaching and sharing what I’ve learned along the way has become a big part of my journey. I’m always excited to help others discover their potential, whether it’s in graphic design or coding. Feel free to check out my projects, and if you ever want to chat about design, tech, or anything in between, don’t hesitate to reach out!
